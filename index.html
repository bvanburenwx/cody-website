Meet Cody

I’m Cody, a Compass real estate agent based in the Triangle. I work with people who want more than a sales pitch—they want someone who will listen, explain the “why” behind the strategy, and stay in their corner from the first conversation to the closing table.

I pay close attention to pricing, neighborhood trends, and timing so you can make decisions that feel good in both the short term and the long run. I’m big on clear communication, realistic expectations, and making sure you never feel rushed or talked over.

At the end of the day, real estate is personal. You’re not just buying or selling a property—you’re setting up the next chapter of your life. I take that seriously.
